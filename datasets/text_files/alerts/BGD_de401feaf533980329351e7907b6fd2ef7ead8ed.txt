A data leak happens when these functions fill in sockaddr data structures used to export socket’s diagnostic information. As a result upto 100 bytes of the slab data could be leaked to a userspace. Impact : Local attackers can exploit these issues to obtain sensitive information that may lead to further attacks. Mitigation : Updates are available. Please check specific vendor advisory for more information.
