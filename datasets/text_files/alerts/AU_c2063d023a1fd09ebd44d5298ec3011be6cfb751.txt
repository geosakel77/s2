Security Hotline 1300 CYBER1 (1300 292 371) 773M accounts affected by 'Collection #1' breach You are here Home About the ACSC View all content View all alerts 773M accounts affected by 'Collection #1' breach The Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC) is aware of a significant data breach affecting 773 million email addresses and usernames. Alert status MEDIUM Titled 'Collection #1', the data breach was made public by Australian cyber security expert Troy Hunt , who identified that a large number of credential lists had been distributed on a known hacking forum. The list included combinations of usernames, hashed and plaintext passwords. This data breach may involve credentials for multiple users of businesses websites/services. 18 Jan 2019 Recommendations to protect 773M accounts affected by 'Collection #1' breach Content written for Individuals & families Small & medium businesses Organisations & critical infrastructure Government View all content First published: 18 Jan 2019 Last updated: 15 Mar 2019 Share on Was this information helpful?
