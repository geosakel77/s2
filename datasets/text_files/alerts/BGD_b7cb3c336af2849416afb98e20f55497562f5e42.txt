A user/process inside a guest could use this flaw to potentially escalate their privileges inside the guest. Impact : An attacker can leverage this issue to gain elevated privileges and perform unauthorized actions which may aid in launching further attacks. Mitigation : Updates are available. Please see the references or vendor advisory for more information.
