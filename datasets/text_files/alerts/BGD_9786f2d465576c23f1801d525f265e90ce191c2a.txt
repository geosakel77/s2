Internet Explorer 9, 10 and 11 are vulnerable. Impact: Attackers can exploit this issue to execute arbitrary code in the context of the currently logged-in user. Failed attacks will cause denial of service conditions. Mitigation: Updates are available. Please check specific vendor advisory for more information.
