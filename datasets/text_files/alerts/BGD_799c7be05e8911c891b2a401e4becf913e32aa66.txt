This was addressed in epan/dissectors/asn1/ros/packet-ros-template.c by validating an OID. Impact : Attackers can exploit this issue to crash the affected application, resulting in denial-of-service conditions. Mitigation : Upgrade to Wireshark 2.2.7 or later.
