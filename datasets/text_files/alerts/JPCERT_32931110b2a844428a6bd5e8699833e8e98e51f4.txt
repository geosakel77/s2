       Vulnerabilities in Adobe Reader and Acrobat (APSB16-09)

        https://www.jpcert.or.jp/english/at/2016/at160012.html


I. Overview

  Multiple vulnerabilities exist in Adobe Acrobat Reader, a PDF file
viewing software, and Adobe Acrobat, a PDF file creation and 
conversion software. As a result, a remote attacker may terminate
Adobe Reader and Acrobat or execute arbitrary code by convincing a 
user to open contents leveraging the vulnerabilities. For more 
information, please refer to the Adobe Systems website.

    Security Updates Available for Adobe Acrobat and Reader
    https://helpx.adobe.com/security/products/reader/apsb16-09.html


II. Affected Products

  Affected products and versions are as follows:

  - Adobe Acrobat Reader DC Continuous (15.010.20059) and earlier
  - Adobe Acrobat Reader DC Classic (15.006.30119) and earlier
  - Adobe Acrobat DC Continuous (15.010.20059) and earlier
  - Adobe Acrobat DC Classic (15.006.30119) and earlier
  - Adobe Acrobat XI (11.0.14) and earlier
  - Adobe Reader XI (11.0.14) and earlier


III. Solution

  Please update Adobe Reader and Acrobat to the latest version listed
below. 

  - Adobe Acrobat Reader DC Continuous (15.010.20060)
  - Adobe Acrobat Reader DC Classic (15.006.30121)
  - Adobe Acrobat DC Continuous (15.010.20060)
  - Adobe Acrobat DC Classic (15.006.30121)
  - Adobe Acrobat XI (11.0.15)
  - Adobe Reader XI (11.0.15)
  
  Acrobat will be updated by starting the products, selecting the
menu "Help (H)", and then clicking "Check for Updates (U)". If an 
update from the menu is not available, please download the latest 
Adobe Reader and Acrobat from the following URL. For more 
information, please refer to the Adobe Systems website.

    Adobe.com - New downloads
    http://www.adobe.com/support/downloads/new.jsp
