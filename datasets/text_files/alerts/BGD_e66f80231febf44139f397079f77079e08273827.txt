The directive calls for all organizations to assess their exposure to the compromise and secure their networks against exploitation. The following SolarWinds Orion versions are affected: Orion Platform 2019.4 HF5, version 2019.4.5200.9083 Orion Platform 2020.2 RC1, version 2020.2.100.12219 Orion Platform 2020.2 RC2, version 2020.2.5200.12394 Orion Platform 2020.2, 2020.2 HF1, version 2020.2.5300.12432 CISA (US Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency) have issued an alert (AA20-352A) to include additional Indicators of Compromise (IoCs) and further mitigation advice.
