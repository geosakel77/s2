During a user-space close of a Netlink socket, it allows attackers to cause a denial of service (use-after-free) or possibly have unspecified other impact. Impact : An attacker can exploit this issue to cause denial-of-service condition. Mitigation : Updates are available. Please check specific vendor advisory for more information.
