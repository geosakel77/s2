Impact : An attacker can exploit this issue to cause a local denial-of-service condition; other attacks may also be possible. Mitigation : Administrators may disable administrative privileges on the Windows machines that have Cisco AMP for Endpoints installed. For information about fixed software releases, consult with vendor.
