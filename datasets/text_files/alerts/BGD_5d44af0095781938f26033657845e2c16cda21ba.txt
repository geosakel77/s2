The vulnerability affects Microsoft Office, including the latest Office 2016 edition running on Windows 10. Impact: Researchers has observed Office documents exploiting CVE-2017-0199 that download and execute malware payloads from different well-known malware families. Mitigation: Updates are available. Please check specific vendor advisory for more information.
