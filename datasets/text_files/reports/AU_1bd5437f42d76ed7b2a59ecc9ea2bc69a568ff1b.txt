Security Hotline 1300 CYBER1 (1300 292 371) ACSC Threat Report 2017 You are here Home About the ACSC View all content View all reports and statistics ACSC Threat Report 2017 This is the third Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC) Threat Report. It continues to reflect the experience, focus, and mandates of the ACSC’s member organisations. This report provides an insight into what the Centre has been seeing, learning, and responding to, focusing on specific areas of change or new knowledge obtained. For the first time, this year’s Threat Report also includes insights into how the ACSC works and highlights some of the ways in which we have both proactively and reactively responded to cyber threats.
